# Taledge AI Scoring Algorithm (World-Class Elite Edition)

The "Fit Score" is driven by what is arguably the most rigorous, complex, and adversarial AI evaluation engine in the world. It transcends simple "right or wrong" answers by mapping cognitive load, linguistic biomarkers, and micro-expression proxies (latency).

Here is the exact scoring matrix the AI evaluates the candidate against on a rigid 0-100 scale:

---

## 1. Domain A: Technical & Cognitive (Weight: 45%)
The AI acts as an adversarial engineering leader, specifically targeting the candidate's threshold for cognitive failure.

- **Cognitive Load Capacity:** Multi-constraint problem solving, Working memory retention (tracking multi-part questions).
- **Adversarial Tech Resilience:** Defense of architecture under cross-examination, Yielding vs. Defending logic (Do they fold under pressure or defend sound architecture?).
- **Systems Architecture Depth:** O(n) trade-off awareness, Failure-state edge case identification, Microservice anti-patterns.
- **Code Quality & Pragmatism:** Algorithmic efficiency, Clean code principles, Over-engineering avoidance.
- **Micro-Expression Proxies:** Latency variance (hesitation detection), Hint dependency penalty, Confidence consistency.

## 2. Domain B: Behavioural & Psychometric (Weight: 40%)
The AI acts as an elite HR Psychologist, bypassing generic STAR method answers to evaluate deep-seated behavioral markers.

- **Advanced Psychometrics (DNLA):** Emotional regulation under adversarial questioning, Cognitive dissonance detection, Dark Triad suppression.
- **Conflict Resolution Depth:** Blame distribution index, De-escalation strategy map, Post-mortem accountability.
- **Linguistic Biomarkers:** Defensive mechanism triggers, Pronoun usage ("I" vs "We" indexing), Calibrated verbosity.
- **Strategic Empathy:** Perspective taking metrics, Stakeholder map understanding.
- **Growth & Neuroplasticity:** Feedback assimilation rate, Fixed vs Growth mindset indicators.

## 3. Domain C: Resume & Foundation (Weight: 15%)
The AI evaluates the geometric overlap between the candidate's stated history and the actual role requirement.

- **Skill Mapping Matrix:** JD overlap percentage, Core vs Tangential skill dilution.
- **Project Reality Index:** Complexity vs Claim alignment, Impact quantification index (Are they fluffing metrics?).
- **Academic Trajectory:** Tier multiplier, Pedigree consistency.
- **Information Density:** Fluff-to-substance ratio, Clarity metric.

---

## The AI Interviewer Persona Engine
To extract these metrics, the AI interviewer uses dynamic **Adversarial Follow-ups**. If a candidate gives a standard rehearsed answer, the AI will combine concepts to increase cognitive load, introduce system failure states, or challenge their ethical boundaries to trigger defensive mechanisms. 

The backend aggregates all 25+ parameters mathematically:
`Fit Score = (Cognitive Avg * 0.45) + (Psychometric Avg * 0.40) + (Foundation Avg * 0.15)`
